# Citation

## NGHGI-Reported LULUCF Emissions (Melo et al. 2026)

Melo, J., et al. (2026). The LULUCF Data Hub: translating global land use emissions estimates into the national GHG inventory framework (Version 3.1.1, 2025 NGHGI release). *Zenodo*. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18352395

Files:
- `timeseries_NGHGI_v3.1` — CO2 fluxes sourced from the National Greenhouse Gas Inventories (NGHGIs) submitted to the United Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC). CO2 fluxes reported by countries in IPCC categories and sub-categories are allocated to the classes Forest (excl. organic soils, excl. HWP), Deforestation (conversion of forest land to other land uses), Other non-forest land uses, Organic soils, and Harvested wood products (HWP), with gaps filled without altering the levels and trends of the reported data. Units: MtCO2/yr. Sign convention: negative = net sink.
